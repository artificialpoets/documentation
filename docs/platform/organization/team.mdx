---
title: Team Management
description: How to invite, manage, and remove team members in your organization, including the difference between Admin and Guest roles and how pending invitations work.
---

The **Team Management** page lists every member of your organization plus any outstanding invitations. From here you can invite new people, change a member's role, remove a member, or revoke a pending invitation.

### Roles

Two roles exist today:

| Role | What it can do |
|------|----------------|
| **Admin** | Full access — manage settings, billing, team, and every site |
| **Guest** | Limited access — view the dashboard but not modify org-level configuration |

Roles render as badges in the table: Admin (orange) and Guest (zinc). Pending invitations also render a Pending badge alongside the proposed role.

### Team list

The table has one row per member or pending invitation, with the following columns:

| Column | What it shows |
|--------|---------------|
| Avatar | The member's profile picture, or their initials when no picture is set |
| Name | Display name, or "Pending Invitation" for unaccepted invites |
| Email | The member's primary email address |
| Role / Status | Role badge for active members; role + Pending for invitations |
| Menu | Per-row actions |

### Inviting someone

Click **Invite person** to open the invite modal. The form requires:

- **Email address** — where the invitation will be sent
- **Role** — Admin or Guest; defaults to Guest

On submit, the invitation is recorded and the new entry appears in the table with a Pending badge. Invitations expire after 3 days. The invitee receives an email with a link to accept and join.

### Per-row actions

The kebab menu on each row exposes different actions depending on row type:

**Active members:**

- **Make Guest** / **Make Admin** — swap the member's role. The label flips based on the member's current role.
- **Remove from organization** — revokes access. The row is removed immediately on success.

**Pending invitations:**

- **Revoke invitation** — cancels the pending invite. The row is removed from the table.

### How team membership relates to sites

Adding someone to the organization gives them access to every site connected under it. There is no per-site team membership — see [Sites](../sites/) for what each member can see once they're in the org. Billing and organization-wide settings — like the brand color set on [Organization Settings](./settings) — are gated to Admins.
