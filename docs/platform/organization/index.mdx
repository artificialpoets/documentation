The **Organization** section is where you configure your tenant in Artificial Poets. An organization is the top-level boundary in the product: every site, every team member, and every invoice belongs to exactly one organization. Switching organizations switches the data you see across every other section of the dashboard.

### What this section covers

| Page | What it covers |
|------|----------------|
| [Organization Settings](./settings) | Identity, contact info, and sidebar branding (color, logo, layout) |
| [Team Management](./team) | Members, pending invitations, role assignment |
| [Billing](./billing) | Invoices, billing address, and Stripe-backed payment methods |

### How organizations relate to sites

Every site in the [Sites](../sites/) list belongs to one organization. When you connect a new site, it inherits the organization context of the user who connected it. Site-level configuration lives in the per-site [Settings tab](../sites/settings); organization-level configuration lives here.

### How team membership relates to access

Adding someone to the organization grants them access to every site under that organization, scoped by their role. There's no per-site sharing today — invite at the org level and use roles to control what each member can do. See [Team Management](./team) for the role taxonomy.
