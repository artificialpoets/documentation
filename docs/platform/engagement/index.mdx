The **Engagement** section reports what users actually do on the site: how many pages they viewed, how long they stayed, how deep they read, and what they searched for. Audience tells you who arrived; Engagement tells you whether they engaged.

| [Total Pageviews](./total-pageviews) | Aggregated pageview volume across the window |
| [Session](./session) | A bounded visit — how sessions begin and end |
| [Pageviews per Session](./pageviews-per-session) | Average page count per session |

### How to read the section

Start with [Engagement Overview](./overview) for the framing. The volume metrics ([Pageview](./pageview), [Session](./session), and friends) establish baselines. The depth metrics ([Bounce Rate](./bounce-rate), [Scroll Depth](./scroll-depth), [Reading Stage](./reading-stage)) reveal how engaged users actually were. The search metrics surface what users came looking for and whether they found it.
